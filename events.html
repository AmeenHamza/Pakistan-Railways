Bhagat Singh was arrested

Lahore during Dussehra in 1927

Bhagat Singh was arrested in connection with a bomb explosion at Lahore during Dussehra in 1927, and was imprisoned in Lahore Railway police station. Original Image credit goes to Indianhistorypics

Khojak Tunnel, Circa 1905.

Album Of Miscellaneous Views, Taken In The 1880's.

This postcard shows some of the team that constructed the Khojak Tunnel in Balochistan, one of the great feats of 19th century engineering in the subcontinent. Almost 2.5 miles in length, numerous European engineers helped in its construction.
